About The Tea Cozy

At The Tea Cozy, we believe that tea is more than just a beverage—it’s an experience. From sourcing the finest organic tea leaves to crafting unique blends, we are committed to delivering high-quality, ethically sourced tea for every occasion.


Our Promise to You

  • Handpicked: Every leaf is carefully selected to ensure the best flavor and quality.
  • Artisanally Curated: Our expert blenders create unique and delightful tea blends.
  • Free Range: We work with tea farms that use natural and sustainable farming practices.
  • Sustainable: Ethical sourcing and eco-friendly packaging are at the heart of what we do.
  • Small Batch: We focus on quality over quantity to ensure every cup is exceptional.
  • Fair Trade: Supporting farmers and ensuring fair wages is part of our commitment.
  • Organic: No artificial flavors, no chemicals—just pure, natural tea.

Our Story

Founded in 2017, The Tea Cozy started as a small family-run tea shop with a passion for authentic and high-quality tea. Over the years, we have expanded to multiple locations, but our mission remains the same: to bring people together over a cup of perfectly brewed tea.


Meet Our Team

Our passionate team of tea enthusiasts, blenders, and baristas are here to make every visit a memorable experience. Whether you’re a seasoned tea lover or new to the world of tea, we’re here to guide you to your perfect cup.


Join Us on Our Tea Journey

Visit one of our cozy locations, explore our online shop, or follow us on social media to stay updated on new blends, events, and special offers!
